What is FlexPlex?

FlexPlex is a Digital signage network where advertisers can deliver their dynamic content to the end users with the ability to reach in ways other mediums simply cannot. It is a unique platform with features like cloud distribution, dynamic real-time content delivery, geo-targeting & performance measurement.

Our main target is to create internet connected Digital Hoarding ecosystem which will be more flexible & effective than existing conservative hoarding system.

Advertisers

Ad Distribution service

Currently advertisers need to search Digital hoardings manually or through Ad-Agencies. With FlexPlex network they can post their Ads to any hoarding within FlexPlex network in just a few clicks.

  • Everything online
  • Less advertisement cost
  • Flexibility to choose duration, exact time & area
  • Targeting based on Age, Sex & different category of people
  • Hyperlocal advertisers can take advantage
  • Measure Ad performance

Publishers

Digital ad subscription service

Publishers just need to install FlexPlex IOT hardware, that's it, the hoarding will be in Network. Publishers can continue with their existing Ads, additionally they can generate more revenue by hosting more Ads from FlexPlex network.

  • No need to search for advertisers or Ad-Agencies
  • Can show more ads, hence more revenue
  • No need to scrutinize the Ads, FlexPlex will do this for you
  • Can lease their space
  • No downtime
  • More transparency
